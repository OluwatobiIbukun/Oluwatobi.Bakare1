A Journey from Food Science to Data Science

I hold a degree in Food Science and Technology, with a pursuit of a master's degree on the horizon. My journey began with a passion for revolutionizing the food industry by integrating artificial intelligence to tackle the pervasive issue of food waste, particularly in Africa.

The staggering levels of food wastage in our society fueled my desire to explore innovative solutions. Driven by this vision, I embarked on a transition into the realm of data science. This decision has proven to be transformative, opening my eyes to the immense potential of data-driven insights and their applications beyond the scope of the food industry. The journey has been both enlightening and captivating, reinforcing my belief in the power of data to effect positive change.

Beyond data science, I harbor aspirations to pursue opportunities for further academic advancement, with a keen interest in attaining a Ph.D. This pursuit aligns with my overarching goal of contributing meaningfully to the intersection of technology, sustainability, and societal impact.
